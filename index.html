About us

We are a bunch of Vilnius University students. Our main goal is to develop a convenient, easy-to-use and motivating to improve environment for every unique person in this world. The environment is used to create, track and complete tasks of your own! You will be able to complete our daily default tasks such as drinking eigth glasses of water a day or create and complete the ones you opt for. We believe that the way we choose is the most engaging one and the most promising in the long- term! Click that ‘Download’ button and let’s rock this journey to greatness together.
